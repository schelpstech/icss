Who We Are

ICSS is a collective of experts from diverse institutions, collaborating to establish a hub dedicated to the authentic teaching, learning, and research of criminology.

Founded in accordance with the Companies and Allied Matters Decree 1 of 1990 of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, the Institute of Criminology and Strategic Studies functions as a professional, educational, and regulatory entity. Its mission is to provide training, education, and research focused on crimes and criminality, ultimately working towards the prevention of criminal acts and tendencies within society.
